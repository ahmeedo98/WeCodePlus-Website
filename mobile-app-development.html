Mobile App Development.

  • Services
  • Mobile App Development
“Mobile is becoming not only the new digital hub, but also the bridge to the physical world. That’s why mobile will affect more than just your digital operations — it will transform your entire business.”

Phones, tablets, and wearables are bigger than ever, and keeping up with the demands for mobile app development requires programmers with experience in languages like HTML5, React Native, and PHP. At WeCodePlus our mobile application development solutions make it easy to quickly build prototypes and ready-to-deploy mobile apps for any device.

Output Includes:
Cross-Platform Development
Any App we create will be compatible on both Apple and Android devices. Using React Native, our team will deliver high performance cross-platform apps that reduces the hassle of dealing with multiple source codes for different platforms.
Backend Services
We build apps of all shapes and sizes here at WeCodePlus, but a common element is that they communicate with servers. Few apps today operate without some sort of Internet connectivity, meaning that they interact with a backend, web services, or APIs. These APIs could be provided by Google, Amazon, Facebook, or comparable third-parties. They also could be APIs that are developed internally.
App Integration
Our development results in power packed apps. We combine the power of the React Native platform with the many App integrations, ranging from social sharing features to the connecting of an app with the Cloud - making your App on par with the best of Native Apps on the market.
App Maintenance
We understand how hard it is to maintain an app on too many platforms. At WeCodePlus we don't just ensure that your app is prepared for both the iOS and Android store but also that it is always kept up to date according to the needs of the mobile users, irrespective of the platform.
Process:
  • Step 1: Research & Concept
    Research & Concept:

    Once you reaches us with an app idea, we perform in-depth analysis on the basis of demographics, behavior patterns, and target audience to refine the idea into a solid basis for an application.

  • Step 2: Wireframing
    Wireframing:

    Based on your requirements, our experienced WeCodePlus UI/UX designers will create the wireframes of the visualized idea that clearly defines the user’s journey.

  • Step 3: Prototyping
    Prototpying:

    We create a prototype for the app that serves as a quick view of how the app works. We use the best industry techniques to design prototypes and assure that things are going in the right direction.

  • Step 4: Design & Development
    Design & Development:

    Following the best mobile app development practices we start the development process. Our unique development capabilities provide a competitive advantage in creating your app.

  • Step 5: Testing
    Testing:
    We rigorously test the performance of the developed app based on Industry’s standards to evaluate it’s responsiveness, scalability, and stability.

  • Step 6: Deployment
    Deployment:
    Once the developed app fulfills your expectations, we take your app live as per your launch plan.
